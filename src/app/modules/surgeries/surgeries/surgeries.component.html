VASCULAR SURGERY (EVLT)

The EVLT is a minimally invasive medical procedure used to treat varicose veins, which are swollen and twisted veins that usually occur in the legs.

In the EVLT, the doctor performs the procedure under anesthesia and inserts a thin optical fiber (400μm or 600μm) into the patient's affected vein through a small incision. Laser energy is delivered into the vein, generating heat that causes the vein walls to collapse and close.

This effectively seals off the varicose vein, improving symptoms and appearance. The recovery is usually quick, and hospitalization is often not required.

  1. Less Pain
  2. Better Cosmetics
  3. No Recurrence

Facial Contouring

Facial contouring is a minimally invasive laser-based procedure that enhances the youthfulness and attractiveness of the face. It utilizes the S1 Pro laser with a 1470nm wavelength to selectively target water and fat in the skin, resulting in fat reduction and skin tightening. The procedure is safe, minimally invasive, and offers various benefits such as affordability, minimal downtime, instant results, and long-term skin improvement. It is suitable for all skin types and provides a scar-free outcome. The facial laser machine used in the procedure is easy to use and ensures a safe and comfortable experience for both practitioners and clients.
